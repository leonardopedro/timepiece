## Remaining Postulates & Future Work (Closing the Loopholes)

The following analytical and probabilistic postulates are modeled as axioms in the current version of the proof:

1. **Concrete Construction of $\Omega_N$**
   - Provide a concrete construction of $\Omega_N \simeq \mathbb{C}^{\pi(N)}$.
   - Define $E_N$ and $Var_N$ using the Bochner integral over the product of $\epsilon$-bump functions.
   - Prove the orthogonality and variance axioms (`exp_X_eq_one`, `X_orthogonal`, and `Var_X_bound`) under this concrete measure.

2. **Moore-Osgood Commutation (`moore_osgood_commutation`)**
   - Prove the limit commutation by showing that the projective limit of the deterministic sequences generated by Chebyshev's inequality over $\Omega_N$ forms a consistent limit trajectory.

3. **Boundary Behavior & Fundamental Theorems**
   - `eta_non_zero_real_axis`: Prove that the Dirichlet $\eta$ function is non-zero on the real axis $x > 1/2$.
   - `jensen_bohr`: Prove that the convergence of a Dirichlet series at a point implies its holomorphic continuation to the right half-plane.
   - `convergent_series_has_no_poles`: Prove that a conditionally convergent Dirichlet series defines a pole-free analytic function.
